Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PhotoMentor ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Chrome Extension.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address and name to identify your account and manage subscriptions.
  • Images: We process images that you explicitly upload or select for analysis.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous metrics about how you use the extension to improve our service.

3. How We Process Images

We do not store your personal photos on our servers permanently.

When you request an analysis:

  • Your image is temporarily processed to extract visual data.
  • We use the Google Gemini API to perform the AI analysis. The image data is transmitted securely to Google for this purpose.
  • Once the analysis is returned to you, the image data is discarded from our processing pipeline.

4. Payments

We use Stripe for payment processing. We do not store or collect your payment card details. That information is provided directly to Stripe whose use of your personal information is governed by their Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: For AI image analysis.
  • Google Auth: For user authentication.
  • Stripe: For payments and subscriptions.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@ropeloozo.com.
